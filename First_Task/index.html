About this page

11th of September,2001 was the day the world was changed forever. Four separate planes carrying innocent civilians were hijacked by 19 al-Qaeda terrorists. Three of these four planes reached their targets: both WTC towers and Pentagon, the fourth plane crashed because the passengers fought back. In total the attacks killed 2,977 and injured over 6,000 people, making 9/11 attacks the most tragic terrorist act in human history. This page was created to provide information about 9/11 attacks: timeline of the attacks can be found in Timeline section, table of the top 10 countries with biggest 9/11 casualties can be found in Casualties section, Subscription section contains form using which you can subscribe to our newsletter, Media section contains images of the attacks.
